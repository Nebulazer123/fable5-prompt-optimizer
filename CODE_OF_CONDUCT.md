# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want participation in this project to be respectful, constructive, and focused on improving the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good faith.
- Give feedback about the contribution, not the contributor.
- Keep discussions relevant to the repository.
- Respect privacy and do not publish private information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Trolling, insults, or repeated bad-faith disruption.
- Publishing private contact information, credentials, or private prompts.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may lead to blocks from the repository.

Report issues through GitHub's reporting tools or by opening a minimal issue asking for a private maintainer contact.
